What shifted

Language

Nunito bold sans → Jost + Fraunces pairing. Fraunces italic for reflections, muted JetBrains Mono for metadata labels. Headings breathe.

Nunito Bold→Fraunces italic

Palette

Dark navy #1B1F3B + gold #F0A500 → warm off-white #FCFBF9 with a clay accent #B5705A. Users can switch to sage or cobalt from the Me screen.

#1A1225→#FCFBF9
#F0A500→#B5705A

Structure

Heavy shadow-rounded cards on every surface → hairline dividers between sections, cards only where they earn their weight. Emoji stripped from the core UI.

Density setting ("Tight / Default / Airy") lets the user pick their rhythm.
